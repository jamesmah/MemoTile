How to play

  1. Find a friend to play this two player game.
  2. Pick a colour each, red or blue.
  3. Choose your favourite Pokémon.
  4. Click on the number of grid tiles to play with. The higher the more difficult!
  5. When you press start, the tiles will appear for a short while. Try to remember the position of the Pokémon that you have chosen.
  6. After they vanish, click on the tiles that your Pokémon are on. If you click on a wrong tile, it will be your opponents turn to play.
  7. Once you open all the tiles with your Pokémon, you win the round!
  • You can click on the crown to reset the scores at anytime.
  • You can click on the settings and change your Pokémon at anytime.
